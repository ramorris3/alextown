All weapons created here are only available to the player. (Enemies have their own separate system of attack configurations.)

The class, rarity, and player level requirements are used in the random loot drop algorithm.

Players will only see weapons from their class, and weapons will be within a certain number of levels away from the player's current level. Rarity reflects how often the weapons will drop.
